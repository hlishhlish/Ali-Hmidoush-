About Me

My name is Ali Hmidous, a Syrian physician who strives to see the world through a broader lens—one that extends beyond medicine and embraces humanity. Witnessing the impact of conflict on people, communities, and health systems profoundly shaped my perspective and inspired me to dedicate my career to serving vulnerable populations.

These experiences taught me that health is more than treatment; it is dignity, security, opportunity, and hope. They also reinforced my belief that every human being deserves to live peacefully and with respect, regardless of where they come from or the circumstances they face.

Through humanitarian action, global health, digital innovation, and research, I aspire to contribute to solutions that create lasting impact and support communities affected by conflict, displacement, and inequality.
